State of art
September - December 2014

During the first project period, we focused on the state of art regarding the 6LowPAN security, including hardware support and software. We could then define a target solution, and test it in the Cooja simulation environment before the PoC implementation.

Proof of Concept
January - February 2015

We have developed and implemented a driver for the AES module of the Zigduino_r2 platform running ContikiOS. In order to ensure the confidentiality of the exchanges between all the nodes, we implemented the protocol Lithe (CoAP) on several platforms, which is a protocol of exchange of secrecy.
